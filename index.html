Chapter IV

Some Guys In London

A vertical micro-comedy sitcom about two London flatmates. Jamie performs, Malik refuses. Ten episodes. In production now.

The premise: Jamie believes identity is something you manage. Malik believes it's something you are. They share a flat. That gap is the engine of every episode.

The pipeline: composite 2D characters on real-feeling 3D plates, animated to recorded audio. Human creative direction throughout. We use AI to do what would otherwise be prohibitively expensive, and we're transparent about which parts are which.
